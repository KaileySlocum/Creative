Five Senses

I put together this video for my MUL 101: Intro to Multimedia class at Lane Community College during Fall term 2019. This assignment was inspired by the question: "What does 5 mean to you?" I decided to create my class project video about the five senses. Although I use these senses every day, I often overlook the blessing of being able to touch, smell, hear, taste, and see. Enjoy!










The Things We Miss When We Rush Through Life

I put together this video for my MUL 101: Intro to Multimedia class at Lane Community College during Fall term 2019. This assignment was my final project, which I put together as a thoughtful reflection about the need to slow down and enjoy life instead of rushing through it.

Bamboo Skewer Bridge

I recorded this video for my ART 117: 3D Design class at Lane Community College. This project required me to build a bridge out of hot glue and bamboo skewers, then to test it by adding weight to it. I learned to work with design constraints.

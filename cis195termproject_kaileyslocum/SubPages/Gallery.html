A Passion for Photography

When I was ten years old, I bought my first point-and-shoot camera. Ever since then I have developed a passion for capturing the world around me through a camera lens. I recently bought a CANON Rebel T7 DSLR, and have enjoyed the new possibilities that its features provide me when I take pictures.
My favorite things to photograph are objects and nature. I especially enjoy close-up photos, and would love to practice more with macro lenses. Spring is one of the best times of year for taking pictures of nature because of the beautiful flowers and blossoms.
I have also enjoyed developing my photography skills so that I can take good family photos for holidays, events, and vacations. Scrapbooking is another of my hobbies, and I love capturing memories these ways!
